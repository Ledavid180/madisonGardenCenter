our story

Madison Garden Center got it's start in a tiny house on Highway 51 in the city of Madison. After 8 years at this location, Nick Thompson, decided it was time to expand and move to it's current location on Madison Avenue, across from Liberty Park.

Being the only garden center in the city of Madison, we focus on providing customer service, quality plant material, durable furniture, weather resistant pottery/concrete, a variety of soils and mulches, etc.

Madison Garden Center has been in business 20+ years and has been going strong ever since. Our staff at Madison Garden Center have 50+ combined years of expertise in gardening and horticulture.

our team

nick thompson

store owner

Nick's passion for gardening and landscaping started in highschool working part-time at a small garden center in Pascagoula, MS, Gallaspy's Store. After finishing his business degree, he received his first post-college job. He worked for BWI, a distributor for pesticides, fertilizers, horticulture and landscaping related items. Nick then went on after leaving BWI to open Madison Garden Center in 1998. He has been the owner to this day! He is a Mississippi Certified Nursery Professional and has over 25 years of green industry experience. Come see Nick for your specific plant needs and for any issues pertaining to lawn and yard maintenance and design.

favorite plant: camellia japonica

favorite product: bird houses
